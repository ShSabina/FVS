Vegetable Mixed Salad

    Ingredients:

  1. 1 package (10 ounces) frozen mixed vegetables
  2. 1 can (15 ounces) kidney beans, rinsed and drained
  3. 1/2 cup chopped celery
  4. 1/2 cup chopped onion
  5. 1/2 cup chopped green pepper
  6. 3/4 cup sugar
  7. 1/2 cup vinegar
  8. 1 tablespoon cornstarch




Direction:

Cook the mixed vegetables on the stove or in the microwave until crisp-tender. Drain; place in a large bowl. Add kidney beans, celery, onion and green pepper. In a saucepan over medium heat, bring sugar, vinegar and cornstarch to a boil, stirring constantly, until thickened. Cool slightly. Pour over vegetables and toss. Refrigerate. Yield: 8 servings.

TOTAL TIME: Prep: 20 min. + cooling

MAKES: 8 servings



Fresh Fruit Salad with Honey Vanilla Yogurt

    Ingredients:

  1. 2 cup plain yogurt
  2. 2 tablespoon good honey
  3. 1/2 teaspoon pure vanilla extract
  4. Seeds scaped from 1/2 vanilla bean, optional
  5. 1/2 orange, juced
  6. 1 banana, sliced
  7. 1/2 pint fresh blueberries
  8. 1/2 pint fresh rasberries
  9. 1 pint fresh strawberries, hulled and cut in half
  10. 1 bunch seedless green grapes, halved






Direction:

Combine the yogurt, honey, vanilla extract, and vanilla bean seeds in a bowl and set aside. Combine the orange juice and banana slices in a separate bowl. Add the berries and grapes and gently mix the fruit mixture together. Spoon the fruit into serving bowls and top with the yogurt.

Yield: 4 to 6 servings.

TOTAL TIME: Prep: 10 min
