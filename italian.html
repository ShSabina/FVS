Italian Salad





    Ingredients:
  • 1 head romaine lettuce, roughly chopped
  • 1/2 head iceberg lettuce, chunked
  • 6 whole pepperoncini
  • 1/2 cup whole black olives
  • 1/2 cup whole red cherry tomatoes
  • 1/2 small red onion, very thinly sliced into circles
  • 1/3 cup grated Parmesan


Directions:

Put the romaine and iceberg greens in a large bowl and toss with the dressing. Top the salad with the pepperoncini, olives, tomatoes and onions. Sprinkle with Parmesan at the end.
